POSH Compliance — In Detail

At Ethos, we strengthen workplace integrity through comprehensive POSH solutions — designing clear policy frameworks, delivering engaging training, and embedding transparent governance systems in line with the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 (commonly called the POSH Act).

POSH Policy Frameworks

We design clear, compliant policies that foster safe and respectful workplaces, in line with the POSH Act.

Training & Awareness Programs

We equip teams and leaders with practical knowledge to uphold dignity and inclusivity, fulfilling the Act's requirement for regular awareness sessions.

Governance & Reporting Systems

We establish transparent processes that strengthen accountability and trust, ensuring compliance with reporting obligations under the Act.

Advisory & Case Handling Support

We provide expert guidance to ensure sensitive matters are addressed with integrity, supporting organisations in conducting fair inquiries.

External Subject Matter Expert (POSH IC Committee)

We serve as independent experts on Internal Committees, as mandated by the Act, bringing credibility and impartiality to decision‑making.

POSH Audit Services

We conduct thorough audits to assess compliance, identify gaps, and recommend improvements for stronger workplace safety and integrity.

The "Ethos" Approach to Compliance & Labour Code

We believe compliance is not just about avoiding risk — it's about building trust. Our advisors ensure organisations stay aligned with evolving labour codes, embed POSH frameworks, and strengthen governance practices. By safeguarding integrity and reducing risk, we help you create workplaces that are safe, ethical, and future‑proof.

Ready to strengthen compliance and governance? Let's protect your organisation with confidence.

For Employees

Know Your Rights

Every employee has rights — the right to a safe workplace, fair treatment, timely pay, and legal protection. Ethos helps you understand these rights and empowers you to exercise them confidently.

What We Help With

Right to Safe Working Conditions

Understanding employer obligations under the Occupational Safety, Health and Working Conditions Code and POSH Act.

Right Against Unfair Dismissal

Knowing when a termination is wrongful and what recourse is available under Indian labour law.

Right to Timely & Correct Pay

Your entitlements under the Payment of Wages Act and Code on Wages — including overtime and deduction limits.

Right to Maternity & Paternity Leave

Full entitlements under the Maternity Benefit Act and applicable state laws for parental leave.

Right to Non-Discrimination

Protection against discrimination based on gender, religion, caste, disability, or pregnancy in the workplace.

Right to Raise Grievances

The formal and informal channels available to you to raise concerns safely and without fear of retaliation.

The "Ethos" Approach to Knowing Your Rights

Knowledge is protection. Many employees endure unfair treatment simply because they don't know that better is both possible and legally required. Our advisors present Indian labour law and workplace rights in plain, actionable language — so you can advocate for yourself effectively, and know exactly when and how to seek further support.

Ready to know and exercise your rights? Let's start the conversation.
